2.  Discussion/Action:  First Street Traffic and Street Parking. 

First Street, between B Street and Aleut Street, has been widened to the west side with a 
concrete curb, gutter, and sidewalk.  The east side of First Street has not been widened and 
has no street improvements.  The City has posted No Parking along the east side of First 
Street, but street parking is allowed along the west side.  
 
When vehicles are parked along the west side of First Street it makes the remaining street 
pavement too narrow for two-way traffic flow.  
 
Recommendation:  Direct staff on this street traffic item. 
 

3.  Discussion/Action:  B Street - Sidewalk Improvements at UPRR Track Crossing. 
 
  The planned ATP/SRTS project sidewalk improvements at the B Street UPRR crossing are 

on hold pending the UPRR permit approval.  UPRR has provided a cost estimate for the 
required track upgrade work and is requesting approval for the proposed cost-share 
agreement. 

 
  Recommendation:  Direct staff on UPRR’s request for a cost-share agreement.
