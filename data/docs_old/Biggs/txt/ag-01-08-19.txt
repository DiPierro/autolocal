2. Discussion/Action:  Emergency Ordinance – Disaster Recovery Structures and Uses. 

As reported to Council last month, the Governor of California issued an Executive Order 
proclaiming a state of emergency regarding the resulting housing crisis in our region due to the 
Camp Fire in Paradise.  Staff has drafted an emergency ordinance in order to prepare for related 
housing requests within the City of Biggs. 

Recommendation:  Motion/Second to introduce and adopt Ordinance No. 416 by reading of 
title only, “AN EMERGENCY ORDINANCE OF THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF 
BIGGS ADDING CHAPTER 14.430 TO THE BIGGS MUNICIPAL CODE REGARDING 
DISASTER RECOVERY STRUCTURES AND USES”.
