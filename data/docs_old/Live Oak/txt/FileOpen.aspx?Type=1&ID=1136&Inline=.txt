City of Live Oak 

Report to City Council 

Meeting Date:  September 18, 2019 

Title:   Resolution: Temporary Reduction of Water Connection, Sewer Connection and 
Drainage Connection Fees 

Location:  Citywide 

Recommendation:   

•  Pass  a  resolution  reducing  on  a  temporary  basis  the  developer  mitigation  fees,  (water 
connection,  sewer  connection,  drainage  connection,  traffic  mitigation,  fire  facility,  police 
facility,  general  buildings,  park  facilities,  recreation  facilities,  corporation  yard  and 
community center building fees) on the next 200 permits for new single-family homes.  

Fiscal Impacts:  The new homes generate additional on-going taxes and revenues with an 
estimated payback period between 1.7 and 2.4 years for each home.  One-time developer 
mitigation fees will total $4,908,000 at the reduced level vs. $6,469,000 at full rates (see attached 
exhibit) for the 200 homes.  

Contact Information:  Aaron Palmer, City Manager (530) 695-2112 

Background: 

At the June 5, 2019 City Council meeting, the City Council provided direction to staff to draft the 
needed documents to temporarily reduce the developer mitigation fees on the next 200 permits on 
new single-family homes.  City staff performed costing models that shows that each new home 
produces additional on-going taxes and revenues that offset the incentives within 1.7 years for 
each house built in CFD 2004.  For homes to be built outside of CFD 2004, the payback is 
estimated at 2.4 years for each home.
